Carnival
Dunk Tank
Carnival's already a good time — but nothing brings people together like watching someone get soaked. We're adding a dunk tank, and yes, we're putting teachers in it.
Student Vendor Market
Want to sell your art, your baked goods, your thrifted fits? Carnival is getting a student market. More ways to be part of it, not just attend it.
Live DJ
No more shuffled playlists. Carnival gets a live DJ so it actually feels like an event worth showing up for.
Photo Booth
Every event gets a dedicated photo setup. Something worth posting — not just a blurry crowd shot.
Spirit Day
Teacher vs. Student Games
Bear Mountain is fun. Bear Mountain where you get to beat your teachers at something? Better. We're making it official — and recruiting the teachers who are actually down for it.
Color Wars
Each grade gets a color. Every competition counts toward a leaderboard. By the end of the day there's one winning grade. Spirit Day should feel like a rivalry, not just a trip.
Junior / Senior Football Game
Permanent School Trophy
The Junior-Senior game needs something to play for. We're creating a real trophy that lives in the school and gets passed between grades every year. History starts now.
Halftime Show
We've got talent in this school. The football game is getting a halftime show — whether that's the jazz ensemble, talent show acts, or something else entirely. The game doesn't stop at halftime, it gets better.
Tiebreaker Round
If the game ends in a tie, it doesn't end there. We're introducing a tiebreaker so there's always a winner — because bragging rights need to mean something.
New Events
Game Night
Board games, cards, maybe some consoles. Low key, low cost, high turnout. The kind of event people actually show up to without being forced.
Trivia Night
Inter-class trivia competition hosted by students. Grades compete, stakes are real, and someone gets to feel like a genius in front of everyone.
Movie Night
Auditorium, big screen, students vote on the movie. Simple idea that nobody's done yet.
Sports Tournament
Basketball, volleyball, ping pong — whatever we have space for. Inter-class bracket, single elimination. Bragging rights on the line.
Every Event
Student Feedback Form
You have opinions about how events go. I actually want to hear them. A feedback form after every major event so next time is always better than last time.
